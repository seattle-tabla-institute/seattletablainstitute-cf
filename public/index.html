Tabla education in Seattle

Seattle Tabla Institute is a music school and community hub.

Tabla education, community programs, and cultural preservation in Seattle.

Seattle Tabla Institute is a nonprofit educational organization serving the Greater Seattle area. We provide tabla classes for youth and adults, community programs, and public performances that preserve Hindustani music and expand access to arts education. Our work builds cultural understanding, supports lifelong learning, and keeps this tradition thriving in Seattle.

Program details

We keep programs structured but flexible so families and adult learners can find a pace that works.

What does a typical class look like?

Classes combine rhythmic exercises, repertoire, and listening practice. Students build technique step-by-step with consistent feedback.

Do you offer hybrid or online options?

Select cohorts can meet online or in a hybrid format depending on the session and student needs. We will confirm availability during enrollment.

How are students placed?

We recommend a cohort based on age, goals, and prior experience. New students can start with an introductory pack before joining a longer series.

Frequently asked

Do I need prior music experience?

No. Our beginner tracks are designed for first-time learners.

Where are classes held?

We teach across the Greater Seattle area, including Seattle and Eastside locations. Exact addresses are shared after enrollment.

How do I enroll?

Start on the Join a Class page and we will follow up with next steps.
