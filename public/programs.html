Programs

Structured tabla learning for youth and adults.

Programs are designed for steady progress, cultural depth, and a supportive community. We place students in cohorts that match their goals and experience.

Adult Tabla Classes (19+)

Welcoming, structured, and beginner-friendly

Learn tabla in a supportive, structured environment designed for adult learners. No prior music experience is required—just curiosity and commitment.
