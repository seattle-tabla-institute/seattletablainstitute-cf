Our mission

We preserve and share North Indian classical music through accessible, high-quality instruction and community events. Our goal is to build lasting connections through rhythm, listening, and cultural understanding.

We welcome students and audience members from all backgrounds who are curious about Hindustani music and the tabla tradition.

What guides our work

  • Respect for cultural lineage
  • Consistent, structured learning
  • Community-centered performances

Teaching philosophy

Tabla is taught through focused technique, repertoire, and listening practice. Students build confidence over time with clear milestones and guidance.

Community impact

Public events and collaborations allow learners and audiences to connect with Hindustani music beyond the classroom.
